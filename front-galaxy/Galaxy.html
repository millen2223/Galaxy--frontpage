The Galaxy

Diverse research

Galaxy is an open visual web environment that provides data research tools for narrowly focused specialized data processes. Fields of work include but do not limit to biochemistry, genetics, computational metabolomics, ecology, mathematics, etc.

Huge & multifunctional

Each galaxy user receives granted free space 250GB and unlimited access to all commonly used data analysis tools universes of :

  • RStudio
  • Interactive Jupiter Notebook
  • Tools for genomics analysis
  • Nanopore tools
  • Tools for genomics analysis
  • Nanopore tools
  • Statistics
  • Chemicaltoobox
...and billions of others

Share and reproduce

In the Galaxy, it is possible to publish results and share a research algorithm with anyone in the community and beyond it. The algorithm workflows that repeat all the procedures can apply to completely different data escaping tedious work. You can follow and edit any of your steps in history and reuse them endlessly.
